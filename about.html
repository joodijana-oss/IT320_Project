About Sanad

A smarter way to connect users with medication support

Sanad is a healthcare support platform designed to make the process of finding medications more organized, transparent, and less stressful for users.

Who we are

What is Sanad?

Sanad is a web-based platform that helps users request medications and receive responses from pharmacies that have them available. Instead of contacting multiple pharmacies one by one, users can submit a structured request and manage the process in one place.

The platform also includes administrative review before requests are shared with pharmacies, helping maintain safety, organization, and reliability throughout the process.

Our Mission

Making medication access more organized

Our mission is to provide a clear digital process that connects users, pharmacies, and administrative review in a way that saves time and improves communication.

Our Vision

A more transparent healthcare support experience

We aim to create a more user-centered system where medication requests are easier to manage, responses are clearer, and the search process becomes less exhausting.
